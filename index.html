Technical Implementation

Video

Video's aspect ratio is 16:9. All footage was shot on an IPhone SE at 720p. In total, 12 video tracks as well as 5 static images were used. Though mostly using OpenShot for editing the minimovie, we made use of a multitude of tools including iMovie for speeding up clips (timelapse) and iOS's default camera app for image stabilization. Transition effects were implemented to seamlessly connect different clips.

Audio

There are 3 main audio tracks in the movie - two voice recordings as well as a song provided for free for our use case according to its license. Additionally, an alarm clock sound was used (BigSoundBank). Tracks were edited and put into the according part of the video using OpenShot. Background soundtrack is changing volume in time to better fit the ongoing videoclip.
