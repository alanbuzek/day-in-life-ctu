THRILLING STORYLINE

"The most accurate depiction of a student's day, ever." - The New York Times

Get up late.

What better way to start the day off than oversleeping?

Get to school, eventually.

It might've not been as smooth but there you are finally, only 2 hours late.

Have a nap...

Within just a few minutes of class, you start to question why you bothered to get up today in the first place.
